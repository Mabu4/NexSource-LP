FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. You can create an account and explore nexsource before adding a payment method. Upgrade when you're ready.

How are plans limited?

Plans are gated by the number of suppliers you manage — not by user seats. Every plan includes unlimited users.

Where is my data hosted?

nexsource is built on Cloudflare's EU infrastructure with object storage for files. All connections are encrypted in transit.

Can suppliers log in themselves?

Not in this version. nexsource is an internal tool for your team — suppliers don't need accounts.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime through the billing portal. Changes are prorated by Stripe.
