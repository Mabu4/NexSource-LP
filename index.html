Features

Everything your supplier base needs — in one tool.

From supplier onboarding through scoring and contracts to purchase orders: NexSource brings your entire procurement and supplier process together.

Supplier master data

One central supplier database for contacts, addresses, VAT IDs, payment terms and status — searchable and filterable in seconds.

Supplier portal

Suppliers maintain their own master data via a secure link — no account needed. Every change reaches your system only after you approve it.

Supplier scorecard

Rate communication, reliability, flexibility, cooperation and problem-solving on a 1–10 scale — with an overall score per supplier.

Contracts & certificates

Manage contracts and certificates (ISO, IFS, BRC, organic) with validity windows. Automatic expiry alerts at 30, 60 and 90 days.

Document management

All supplier documents secure and organized in one place — per supplier, EU-hosted and available to your whole team at any time.

Team, roles & dashboard

Unlimited users with roles (owner, manager, member). The dashboard surfaces pending approvals, expiring documents and recent activity at a glance.

Compliance

Compliance that runs in the background.

Supply-chain due diligence, sanctions, VAT IDs and certificates: NexSource checks and documents automatically, so you're on the safe side at every audit.

Due-diligence ready

Document supplier due-diligence — increasingly required as larger customers vet your supply chain.

Sanctions screening

Automatic checks against EU, OFAC and UN sanctions lists on every supplier search.

VAT ID (VIES) checks

EU VAT IDs are validated directly through the official VIES system.

Audit-proof

Certificates, contracts and approvals documented end to end — with a full status history.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial — no risk, no credit card required. You only pay once you decide on a plan.

How does AI supplier discovery work?

You describe the supplier you're looking for in a single sentence. NexSource searches the web, suggests matching suppliers and vets each one automatically: VAT ID via VIES, sanctions lists (EU/OFAC/UN) and other trust signals — summarized in a trust score from 0–100. Add a matching result as a supplier with one click.

Can suppliers contribute themselves?

Yes. Through the supplier portal your suppliers get a secure self-service link — no account needed. They update their master data and confirm orders there. Every change reaches you as an approval request; nothing changes without your review.

Does NexSource support supply-chain due diligence?

NexSource helps you document due-diligence obligations: automatic sanctions screening, VAT ID validation, certificate management and a complete status history. This is especially relevant when larger customers vet your supply chain and ask for evidence.

How are plans limited?

Plans are gated by the number of suppliers and AI searches per month — not by user seats. Every plan includes unlimited users with roles (owner, manager, member).

Where is my data hosted?

NexSource runs on EU infrastructure including object storage for files and is GDPR-compliant. All connections are encrypted in transit.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime through the billing portal. Changes are prorated by Stripe.
